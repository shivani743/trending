About us

Why Us ?

Aaditya Wealth is a trusted & well reputed Financial Advisor in India since 2007. It is one of the Best leading stock broking firm & fastest Growing Investment Advisor providing technology led best capital market services in Pan India. We are the National franchise partner of Angel Broking Ltd.
Angel Broking Limited (formerly known as Angel Broking Private Limited) is a member of the Bombay Stock Exchange (BSE), National Stock Exchange (NSE), Metropolitan Stock Exchange of India (MSEI), and NCDEX & MCX, also registered as a Depository Participant with CDSL.
As a stock broking company, Aaditya Wealth brings you 15+ years of rich experience in serving diverse customer base of investors and traders. Over the last few years, we have been able to build a great community of traders and investors who strongly feel the need for the change in the capital market. So far, we have a strong professional team powering more than 5000 customers to trade seamlessly through innovative products, digital platforms and software. We’re the part of Angel Broking Limited who is having handful of stockbrokers in India that can proudly say that we don’t have any conflict of interests with our clients. With each new development, we feel like our journey has just begun.
